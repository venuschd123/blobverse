Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Blobverse ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our game.

1. What We Collect

Blobverse collects the minimum data needed to operate the global leaderboard:

  • Display name — a name you choose (max 14 characters). Not your real name unless you choose to enter it.
  • Game scores and stage progress — your high score, current stage, and timestamp when submitted to the leaderboard.
  • Country code — derived from your browser's language settings, displayed as a flag emoji on the leaderboard. We do not collect your IP address or precise location.

2. What We Store Locally On Your Device

Your game progress (achievements, coins, stars, owned skins, settings) is stored entirely on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and we cannot access it.

3. What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect your real name, email, phone number, or any personally identifying information.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party tracking.
  • We do not display ads (in this version).
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties.

4. Where Data Is Stored

Leaderboard data (display name, score, stage, country flag) is stored on Supabase, a third-party database provider. Their privacy policy is at supabase.com/privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

Blobverse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data on the leaderboard
  • Request deletion of your data from the leaderboard
  • Stop using the game at any time and clear local progress via Settings → Reset All Progress

To make a request, email us at [your-email-here].

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by an updated date above.
